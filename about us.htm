Preface

Time and the world do not stand still, changes are the law of nature and those who look only into the present are sure to miss the future. Today it is a proven fact that education which aims at preparing students for life is a very changing process because when the children reach the age for which they have been preparing the knowledge they have gained proves to be absolute.
So today's education must aim at not preparing but equipping the child for living.

About School

The school came into existence on 24 December 2005 with a mere strength of 6 students with a vision to spread quality education. Since then the school has completed many years of dedicated service to the children of Hazaribag. The school is an English medium, Co-educational institution, affiliated to C.B.S.E, New Delhi (Singhani Branch)/ Affiliated to CEE., New Delhi (Bara Bazar Branch).


We wish to mold the students into dynamic personalities in each and every sphere of life, such that once they pass out of school, they will be an asset to society.

Aim and Objective

We aim at a vigorous pursuit and
excellence in academics, games, and character-building by providing an
all-round meaningful and liberal education. Our emphasis is to initiate and maintain strict discipline in every individual.

~To develop a sense of responsibility, cooperation, generosity, and obedience.
~To develop leadership quality and inculcate the dignity of labor.
~To develop an ethical, spiritual, and altruistic attitude towards life.

School Office
